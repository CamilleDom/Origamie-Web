The preliminary base

Known as the crane base, is one of the frequently used fundamental techniques.


Pinwheel base folds

The water bomb base, also called the pinwheel base folds, folds diagonally to transform a square into a four-pointed star. This initial method forms a triangle, used in particular to make origami boxes.


Kite base folds

The base of the kite is one of the founding bases of origami. No matter your level, you've probably come across it at least once!


Bird base folds

Used to create bird models, this base is often the transformed square base with additional folds to form the body and wings.


Fish base folds

The base of the fish forms the basis for creating the characteristic shape of an origami fish.
